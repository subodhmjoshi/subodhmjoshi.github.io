Subodh Joshi

Welcome to my website!
I am a researcher working in the general area of scientific computing. I am currently working as a postdoctoral researcher at IMB (university of Bordeaux) and INRIA Bordeaux Sud-Ouest research center (team CARDAMOM) in France. I obtained my Ph.D. from the department of Aerospace Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay under the guidance of Prof. Avijit Chatterjee. I am particularly interested in the higher-order numerical schemes and different solution strategies for conservation laws. Some of the applications include shallow water dynamics, Gasdynamics, aeroacoustics and highway traffic modeling. Apart from research, I enjoy reading, photography, trekking and pencil sketching.
Feel free to take a look at my publications and projects. Lastly, you may find something interesting in the links section. Have a good day!
