Welcome to my website!

I am a researcher working in the general area of scientific computing. I am currently working as a postdoc fellow at the department of Computational and Data Sciences (CDS), Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore. Prior to this, I worked as a postdoctoral researcher at IMB (university of Bordeaux) and INRIA Bordeaux Sud-Ouest research center (team CARDAMOM) in France and before that as a Project Research Scientist at ME, IIT Bombay. I obtained my Ph.D. from the department of Aerospace Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay under the guidance of Prof. Avijit Chatterjee.

I am particularly interested in different solution strategies for conservation laws. My ongoing research focuses on physics-informed Machine Learning, data-driven fluid dynamics and simulation based Digital Twins. Some of the application areas of my research include shallow water dynamics, Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD), Gasdynamics, Computational Aeroacoustics (CAA) and highway traffic modeling. In addition to this, my research interests include high performance computing, higher-order accurate methods for CFD and uncertainty quantification. Apart from research, I enjoy reading, photography, trekking and pencil sketching.

Feel free to take a look at my publications and projects. Lastly, you may find something interesting in the links section. Have a good day!
