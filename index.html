Welcome!

Welcome to my website.
My name is Subodh Joshi. I am a researcher working in the general area of scientific computing. I obtained my Ph.D. from the department of Aerospace Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay. I am currently working as a project research scientist at IIT Bombay. I completed my M.Tech. and Ph.D. under the guidance of Prof. Avijit Chatterjee. I am particularly interested in the higher-order numerical schemes and different solution strategies for conservation laws. My Ph.D. work includes development of a multilevel algorithm for efficient simulation of linear propagating waves using higher-order accurate schemes. In addition, I also work on traffic modeling and numerical simulation of traffic flows. Apart from research, I enjoy reading, photography, trekking and pencil sketching.
