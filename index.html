Welcome to my website!

I am a researcher working in the general area of scientific computing and machine learning. I am currently working as an AI researcher at Shell. Prior to this, I was working as a C. V. Raman postdoc fellow at the department of Computational and Data Sciences (CDS), Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore (2020-22), as a postdoctoral researcher at IMB (university of Bordeaux) and INRIA Bordeaux Sud-Ouest research center (team CARDAMOM) in France (2019-20) and before that as a Project Research Scientist at ME, IIT Bombay (2018-19). I obtained my M.Tech. and Ph.D. dual degree from the department of Aerospace Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay under the guidance of Prof. Avijit Chatterjee.

I am particularly interested in different solution strategies for conservation and balance laws. My ongoing research focuses on physics-informed Machine Learning, data-driven fluid dynamics and simulation based Digital Twins. Apart from research, I enjoy reading, photography, trekking and pencil sketching.

Feel free to take a look at my publications and projects. Lastly, you may find something interesting in the links section. Have a good day!
